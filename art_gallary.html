ART GALLARY

This art gallary showcase the historic art-works done by legendary artists showcasing the evolution of artistic expression through the ages.


MONA LISA

The Mona Lisa is a 16th-century portrait painted by the great Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci. The painting is famous for its enigmatic smile, which has fascinated art enthusiasts for centuries.


THE STARRY NIGHT

The Starry Night is a oil painting masterpiece by the Dutch post-impressionist artist Vincent van Gogh. The artwork depicts a swirling night sky filled with stars over a quiet village, the view from Van Gogh's asylum room. The painting is renowned for its vibrant colors and emotional intensity.


THE SCREAM

The Scream is an iconic expressionist painting created by the Norwegian artist Edvard Munch. The artwork portrays a figure with an agonized expression against a tumultuous sky, symbolizing human anxiety. The Scream has become a symbol of psychological turmoil.


THE GREAT WAVE OF KANAGAWA

The woodblock print is a renowned classical japanese art, depicting a massive wave threatening boats near Mt. Fuji,symbolizing nature's power


THE LAST SUPPER

The Last Supper is a late 15th-century mural painting by Leonardo da Vinci. It depicts the moment when Jesus announces that one of his disciples will betray him. The composition and emotional intensity of the figures make it one of the most studied and revered artworks in history.
